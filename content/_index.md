# Ariful Amin

Machine Learning Engineer & Researcher

I build **machine learning systems for healthcare and real-world deployment**, with **6+ years of industry experience** across **health-tech** and **industrial analytics**. My current work at **Malmö University** focuses on **interpretable biomedical time-series learning**, and my broader interests include **medical imaging**, **multimodal AI**, **LLMs**, and **generative AI**.

My technical stack includes **C/C++**, **Python**, **PyTorch**, **FastAPI**, **AWS**, **Docker**, **wearable software**, and **production ML pipelines**. I am pursuing a **Master’s in Applied Data Science** at **Malmö University**, supported by the **Swedish Institute Scholarship for Global Professionals**. Before that, I completed my **BSc in Computer Science and Engineering** at the **University of Dhaka**.
