My research interests sit at the intersection of **interpretable machine learning**, **biomedical AI**, **multimodal learning**, and **real-world deployment**.

## Current thesis work

- **Master’s thesis, Malmö University:** *Inherently Interpretable ML for Epileptic Seizure Prediction Using ECG Signals*
- Focused on prototype-based and attention-driven deep learning methods that remain clinically meaningful while preserving predictive performance

## Ongoing academic research

- **Research Assistant, Malmö University (Dec 2025 - Present):** contributing to an EU-funded project on inherently interpretable deep learning for biomedical time-series
- Working on seizure-prediction pipelines that emphasize transparency, usability, and model trustworthiness in healthcare settings

## Earlier research and research-driven projects

- Research-oriented applied ML work in **multimodal skin disease detection** and **radar-based people counting**
