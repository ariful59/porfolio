At Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh, I contributed to the **HeartWise** smartwatch application in collaboration with Samsung Research America. The work focused on **remote cardiac rehabilitation**, **real-time wearable health monitoring**, and reliable synchronization across smartwatch and mobile workflows.

My contributions included **C++/Tizen wearable development**, **BLE-based data synchronization**, exercise tracking, Six-Minute Walk Test support, multilingual features, self-update mechanisms, and production debugging for release-quality health features.

Samsung's public HeartWise ecosystem includes a smartwatch, mobile app, and clinician dashboard for home-based cardiac rehabilitation.
